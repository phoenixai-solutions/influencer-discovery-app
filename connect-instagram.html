Step 1
Open “Social connections” in Phoenix
Inside Phoenix Influence

We start in your Aruba workspace. Under brand settings you’ll see a simple Social connections screen for Facebook & Instagram.

In the call, we click this button together to launch the Meta login.
Step 2
Approve access on Meta’s screen
Meta / Facebook login

You log in with your own Facebook account, pick the official Aruba Page, and confirm that Phoenix can read content and insights.

Continue as Aruba Tourism Admin
Choose the Page and Instagram account Phoenix Influence can access. Permissions are used only for analytics and reporting.
This is Facebook’s own secure screen. Phoenix never sees your password – Meta simply returns a token confirming you’ve approved access.
Step 3
Connection confirmed & Stories tracked
Back in Phoenix

Once approved, Phoenix shows a connected status. From this point on, any Story that tags @arubatourism can be captured and tied to your campaigns.
